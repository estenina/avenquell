FAQ

Questions premium buyers usually ask first.

Is this AI content?

AI may be part of the production layer. The offer is direction-first creative production: strategy, direction, production, review, and use-case mapping.

Do you manage social media?

Avenquell Studio is not a default SMM agency. Posting and full social media management are included only if scoped.

How much does it cost?

Projects typically start around $5K. The Monthly Creative Content System starts from $9.5K/month with a 3-month minimum.

Can you make our exact product?

Product accuracy depends on references, product complexity, and intended use. Physical product may be required when accuracy matters.

How many revisions are included?

Avenquell Studio typically includes 2 rounds of revisions after approved direction, unless the proposal states otherwise.

Do we get prompts or raw files?

Final delivery includes approved assets in agreed formats. Internal prompts, raw generations, rejected outputs, and tool workflow are not included unless explicitly agreed.
