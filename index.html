FAQ

Questions premium buyers usually ask first.

Is this AI content?

AI may be part of the production layer. The offer is direction-first creative production: strategy, direction, production, review, and use-case mapping. The direction is always human.

How much does it cost?

Projects typically start around $5,000. Monthly Creative Content Systems begin around $9,500/month with a 3-month minimum, depending on scope and complexity.

Can you make our exact product?

Product accuracy depends on references, product complexity, and intended use. Physical product or studio-grade references may be required when accuracy matters.

Do we get the prompts or raw files?

Final delivery includes approved assets in agreed formats. Internal prompts, raw generations, rejected outputs, and tool workflows are not included unless explicitly scoped.
