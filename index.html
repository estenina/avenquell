FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

A few clear answers about how Avenquell works, what is included, and what clients should expect before starting.

Is this AI content?

AI may be part of the production layer. The offer is direction-first creative production: strategy, direction, production, review, and use-case mapping.

Do you manage social media?

Avenquell is not a default social media management agency. Posting, captions, scheduling, and community management are included only if they are specifically scoped.

How much does it cost?

Projects typically start around $5,000. Monthly Creative Content Systems begin around $9,500/month with a 3-month minimum, depending on scope and complexity.

Why do monthly systems start at $9.5K?

Monthly systems include creative direction, production planning, AI-assisted asset creation, human review, use-case mapping, organized delivery, and recurring visual consistency.

Can you make our exact product?

Product accuracy depends on references, product complexity, and intended use. Physical product or studio-grade references may be required when accuracy matters.

How many revisions are included?

Avenquell typically includes two rounds of revisions after the direction is approved. Direction changes after approval are scoped as change requests.

Can you guarantee sales or ROAS?

No. Avenquell does not guarantee sales, ROAS, followers, bookings, or campaign performance. We create campaign-ready creative assets with clear use cases.

Do you work with beauty and skincare brands?

Yes. Beauty and skincare are a strong fit for Avenquell, especially when the brand needs controlled editorial visuals, launch assets, product stories, or monthly content systems.
